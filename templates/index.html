About Me

Hello! My name is Brodie Berger. I am a graduate student in Computer Science in the Southern NJ/Greater Philadelphia area. I specialize in software engineering and data science projects.

I started coding in middle school, making small scripts and game mods. By high school, I was creating full projects. Since then, I've built web apps, machine learning models, and made open-source contributions that put my skills into something worthwhile.

Outside of academics, I love building projects around my interests. My favorite part of development is seeing an idea come to life as a product I'm proud to show off to others.
