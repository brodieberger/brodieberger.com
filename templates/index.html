About Me

Hello! My name is Brodie Berger. I am a current graduate student studying Computer Science in the southern NJ/Greater Philadelphia area. If you are interested in my work and skills, feel free to skip to the projects section or view my resume.

For a little information about me, I first began programming when I was in middle school and quickly fell in love with it. At this point I lacked the knowledge and education to put anything substantial together, mainly sticking to small scripts or modifications to existing programs. It wasn't until high school that I was able to think critically enough to plan, design, and work through a project to the very end.

At Kean University, studying Computer Science was the obvious choice. I chose to focus my undergraduate and graduate studies on software engineering, as building and optimizing software was something that interested me early on.

Outside of academics, I love putting together projects around my interests, with my biggest one being gaming. I usually choose to build things that I would love to use myself, and expectedly, these end up being the things I am most excited to show off. Taking a step back from your work and seeing the project take shape into something you're proud of is one of the most gratifying things about this field.
