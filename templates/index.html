About Me

Hello! My name is Brodie Berger. I am a graduate student in Computer Science in the Southern NJ/Greater Philadelphia area. I specialize in software engineering and data science projects.

I started coding in middle school, making small scripts and game mods. Since then, I've built web apps, machine learning models, and made open-source contributions that put my skills into something worthwhile.

Outside of academics, I love building projects around my interests. My favorite part of development is seeing an idea come to life as a product I'm proud to show off to others.

My most recent work experience was a 2025 summer internship at Virtua Health's Data Science department. At this internship, During this time, I saw firsthand how a healthcare organization uses software engineering and data science in real projects. At Virtua, I got to experience the full lifecycle of a software project. From the first idea and conversations with stakeholders, to the planning, development, and deployment on a production Azure server.
